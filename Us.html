FAQs

Is a phone line necessary?

A phone line is not necessary. The phone with the audio guestbook is entirely self-contained.

Should I bring a power point with me?

When fully charged, the built-in battery of the Audio Guestbook Telephone lasts for a week. In the worst scenario of a battery failure, a power bank can be utilized.

Can I add a personalized greeting?

Yes, you are welcome to leave a message on the phone at the event or send us a personalized greeting in mp3 format

How will my visitors be able to operate the audio guestbook phone?

We have polite guidelines on the table and a phone to encourage people to leave messages.

How long can a single message be?

The Audio Guestbook Telephone has no time limit and will end recording as soon as the call is ended.

Maximum amount of messages my visitors may leave?

You should be safe because a telephone can store messages for up to 120 hours.

When should I make my reservation?

The sooner you order, the better, as we only have a limited quantity of units available.

How much does hiring cost?

We charge R2500 per event
